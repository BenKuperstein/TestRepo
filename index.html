TeloScope

Real-Time News Intelligence from Telegram

The Problem

  • • News is fragmented across multiple channels
  • • Telegram is a key source for real-time, unfiltered news – but it's chaotic
  • • Misinformation and unverified content are rampant
  • • Users lack tools to understand what's happening now and which sources to trust

The Solution: TeloScope

  • • A Telegram-powered client that:
    • — Aggregates news from selected Telegram groups
    • — Uses AI to unify similar stories into evolving topics
    • — Rates credibility of both sources and stories
    • — Visualizes news with dynamic maps, timelines and graphs

Key Features

  • • AI-Powered Topic Clustering
  • • Real-Time Source Credibility Scoring
  • • Auto-Summaries of Ongoing Events
  • • Visual Dashboards: Graphs, Maps, Trends
  • • Community-Driven Feedback and Curation

Why We're Different

  • • Telegram-Centric: Taps into the fastest-growing real-time news ecosystem
  • • Smart Filtering: Filters noise using NLP and user feedback
  • • Visual Context: See the "story arc" unfold across time and geography
  • • AI + Crowd: Combines machine intelligence and human input

Market Potential

  • • Journalists, analysts, activists, and informed citizens
  • • Over 700M monthly Telegram users and growing
  • • Rising demand for trustworthy, real-time news
  • • Expandable to other platforms (X, Reddit, WhatsApp)

MVP Roadmap

  1. Telegram ingestion and clustering of news topics
  2. Basic AI summaries per topic
  3. Source trust scoring (simple metrics)
  4. Interactive web/mobile dashboard
